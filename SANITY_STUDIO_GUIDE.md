- **Slug** - URL-friendly version (auto-generates from title)

**Optional but Recommended:**

- **Main Image** - Click to upload a featured image

### **Step 3: Write Content in Body**

The Body field is a rich text editor:
- **Normal text** - Just start typing
- **Headings** - Click the style dropdown, select H2, H3, etc.
- **Bold/Italic** - Use the formatting buttons
- **Lists** - Click bullet or numbered list buttons
- **Links** - Highlight text, click link button

### **Step 4: Publish**
1. Click the **"Publish"** button in the bottom right corner
2. Your post is now live!
